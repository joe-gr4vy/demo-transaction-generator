Buyers & vaulting

  
When enabled, the script creates a pool of buyers at startup and vaults one card per buyer via client.payment_methods.create(). Both calls use external_identifier so they're idempotent — re-running the script against the same instance will return existing buyers and payment methods rather than creating duplicates.

  
Each transaction is then split between returning customers (uses the buyer's vaulted payment_method_id) and new customers (uses raw card details with store=True, which vaults the card and associates it with the buyer for future runs). The split is controlled by the returning customer % slider.

  
TipA 60-80% returning customer rate is realistic for most subscription or marketplace merchants. Lower it for e-commerce with lots of first-time buyers.
